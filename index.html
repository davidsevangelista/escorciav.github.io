Welcome to my website!!! I am a Colombian engineer interested on computer vision. I am a PhD student at KAUST (Saudi Arabia) advised by Prof. Bernard Ghanem. I am working on the development of algorithms based on machine learning to understand the enormous amount of visual information.

I received my Master degree in Electronics from Universidad del Norte in 2014, under the supervision of Prof. Juan Carlos Niebles.
